Tips for Finding the Right Pet

  • Choose the type of pet you and your family will be comfortable with.
  • Assess your space.
  • Plan ahead for per care.
  • Consider time management.
  • Look for signs of good health.
  • Read your adoption contract carefully.
  • Take your new furry friend for a veterinary exam.
